How to Play

Quick Tips:

  • Pause a game at any time and resume it later from history.
  • Use the menu in the top right to jump to previous games or manage dictionaries.
  • Player suggestions will appear as you type to avoid duplicates.
  • Tap a blank tile in the word entry area to toggle it blank (score 0).
  • The tile bag icon in the top bar shows remaining tiles. Tap it for a detailed breakdown.

Playing a Turn:

  1. Select a starting square on the board for your word.
  2. Choose a direction (ACROSS or DOWN). The word entry box will automatically fill with any existing intersecting letters.
  3. Type your word into the text box. The score preview and tile display will update in real-time. Tap any empty tile in the tile display to mark it as a blank tile (score 0).
  4. Ensure your word is valid and connected to existing words (after the first turn).
  5. Tap Submit Turn to record your score.

Game Management:

Use the play/pause icon in the top bar to:

  • Pause Game: Save your current game to resume later from the history screen.
  • Finish Game: End the current game and declare a winner.
  • Abandon Game: Delete the current game and all its turns permanently.

Dictionary Validation:

Enable dictionary validation on the setup screen or during a game (toggle in the board section) to ensure all submitted words are valid according to the active dictionary.
